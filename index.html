Project Neptune — Pneumatic Cobot Workcell Safety Assessment

ISO 13849-1Functional SafetyRisk Assessment
+

Conducted an ISO 13849-1 safety assessment of a pneumatic collaborative robot workcell featuring a Category 3 dual-channel safety architecture. Calculated MTTFd, DCavg, and PFHd (2.47 × 10⁻⁷/h) to verify compliance with Performance Level d (PLd) and SIL 2 requirements.

Determined a minimum safety distance of 218 mm with a total system response time of ≤100 ms per ISO 13855, and proposed downstream pressure monitoring and automated valve testing to support future PLe/SIL 3 compliance.

4-DOF RRRP AOI Robotic Manipulator

SolidWorksSimscape MultibodyControl Systems
+

Designed and simulated a 4-DOF RRRP robotic manipulator for Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) using SolidWorks, MATLAB Simulink, and Simscape Multibody. Developed inverse kinematics and smooth 3D trajectory planning across 8 inspection waypoints using polynomial interpolation and homogeneous transformation matrices.

Implemented a PD controller (Kp = Kd = 5) with gravity and Coriolis compensation for accurate, stable trajectory tracking — achieving collision-free motion with minimal tracking error throughout a 14-second simulation.
